## 4. Fees and Payment

4.1 **Base Fee.** In consideration of the Services, the Customer shall pay the Provider a base fee of **USD $245,000** (Two Hundred Forty-Five Thousand United States Dollars) per annum (the "**Base Fee**"), payable in equal quarterly installments of USD $61,250 in advance.

4.2 **Implementation Fee.** A one-time implementation fee of **USD $35,000** shall be payable within fifteen (15) days of the Effective Date.

4.3 **Monthly Maintenance Fee.** In addition to the Base Fee, the Customer shall pay a monthly maintenance fee of **USD $7,500** for ongoing platform support, security patching, and availability monitoring, invoiced on the first Working Day of each calendar month.

4.4 **Travel and Expenses.** Pre-approved travel and out-of-pocket expenses shall be reimbursed at cost, provided that (i) individual expenses exceeding USD $1,500 require prior written approval from the Customer, and (ii) supporting receipts are submitted within thirty (30) days of the expenditure.

4.5 **Payment Terms.** All invoices shall be payable within **NET 30 days** from the invoice date. Payments shall be made in United States Dollars by wire transfer to the bank account designated in writing by the Provider.

4.6 **Late Payment.** Any amount not paid when due shall accrue interest at a rate of **1.5% per month** (or 18% per annum), or the maximum rate permitted by applicable law, whichever is lower, from the due date until paid in full. In addition, the Provider reserves the right to suspend Services if any undisputed amount remains unpaid for more than thirty (30) days after the due date.

4.7 **Taxes.** All Fees are exclusive of applicable taxes, duties, and levies, which shall be the responsibility of the Customer, except for taxes imposed on the Provider's net income.

4.8 **Annual Price Adjustment.** The Provider may increase Fees for each Renewal Term by no more than the lesser of (a) the change in the U.S. Consumer Price Index for All Urban Consumers (CPI-U) over the preceding twelve (12) months, or (b) **6% per year**. Any increase shall be notified to the Customer at least ninety (90) days before the start of the applicable Renewal Term.

## 5. Term and Termination

5.1 **Initial Term.** This Agreement commences on the Effective Date and shall continue for an initial period of **twenty-four (24) months**, ending on **February 28, 2027** (the "**Initial Term**"), unless earlier terminated in accordance with this Section 5.

5.2 **Renewal Term.** Upon expiration of the Initial Term, this Agreement shall automatically renew for successive twelve (12) month periods (each a "**Renewal Term**"), unless either Party provides written notice of non-renewal at least **sixty (60) days** before the end of the then-current term.

5.3 **Termination for Convenience.** Either Party may terminate this Agreement without cause at any time after the Initial Term by providing the other Party with at least **ninety (90) days'** prior written notice.

5.4 **Termination for Cause.** Either Party may terminate this Agreement immediately upon written notice to the other Party if:
(a) the other Party commits a material breach of this Agreement and fails to cure such breach within thirty (30) days of receiving written notice;
(b) the other Party becomes insolvent, files for bankruptcy protection, or has a receiver appointed over substantially all of its assets; or
(c) the other Party ceases to conduct business in the ordinary course.

5.5 **Effect of Termination.** Upon termination or expiration of this Agreement:
(i) the Customer shall pay all undisputed amounts accrued up to the effective date of termination within fifteen (15) days;
(ii) each Party shall return or destroy all Confidential Information of the other Party within thirty (30) days;
(iii) Sections 6 (Confidentiality), 7 (Intellectual Property), 9 (Indemnification), 10 (Limitation of Liability), and 12 (Governing Law) shall survive termination.

## Schedule B — Service Level Agreement (SLA)

**Availability Target:** The platform shall be available 99.9% of the time, measured monthly, excluding scheduled maintenance windows notified at least 72 hours in advance.

**Service Credits:** If availability in any calendar month falls below the target, the Customer shall be entitled to service credits as follows:
- Below 99.9% but at or above 99.5%: 10% of that month's maintenance fee
- Below 99.5% but at or above 99.0%: 25% of that month's maintenance fee
- Below 99.0%: 50% of that month's maintenance fee

Service credits are the Customer's sole remedy for availability failures and shall be applied against future invoices.
